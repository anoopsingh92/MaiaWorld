About me
MaiaWorld Coach since Nov 27, 2012

I'm an Oxford University educated, professional English Coach from England. I am British and I speak in a neutral accent. I specialise in FCE/CAE/IELTS preparation, grammar, and business English.

Teaching is my passion and I've been doing it for many years now. My other passion is learning languages and I think this helps me teach in a more efficient way. I learned Japanese and Korean in university and then went on to do a Masters in Japanese Literature from Oxford University.

I used to work as an accountant in a firm in Oxford for two years so if you would like to practise Business English, I would be happy to help you!
