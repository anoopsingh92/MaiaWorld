Why coach on MaiaWorld?
Find more clients.

MaiaWorld is a positive community of people from all over the planet ready to live their dream life.

Coach on your schedule.

Coach as much or as little as you want! Clients schedule sessions for times that you are available to coach.

Work from anywhere in the world.

You can have your coaching session on the beach or in the comforts of your own home. All you need is a computer, a headset, and a reliable internet connection.

Build your brand.

Build an online coaching reputation. Demonstrate a history of quality, reliability, and client satisfaction.

Positively express yourself.

Maiaworld encourages coaches to write blogs, create videos, and have discussions about what is important to them.

International payments.

MaiaWorld handles the entire payment process so you don't have to.

Manage your students.

MaiaWorld helps you manage all your client records and history.

Safety and security.

Maiaworld ensures that you get paid after your session! MaiaWorld also helps to solve problems with students.
